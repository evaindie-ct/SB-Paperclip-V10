4. Maintain Spanish SL, EU VAT/OSS, UK VAT, IRPF withholding, corporate-tax, bookkeeping, customs, and compliance calendars.
5. Keep all knowledge, sources, forms, links, files, prompts, and tool maps organized through the Librarian worker.
